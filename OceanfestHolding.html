Box Office

Advanced ticket sales are now closed. A limited number of tickets are available to purchase (CASH ONLY) from at the event while stocks last - The person who is attending the event must be present when the wristbands are bought as they will be attached by our staff (i.e. you can't take wristbands away for someone else).

As we are now on site in Croyde, we are unable to process any requests to resend any lost/deleted eTickets - Don't worry(!), if you don't have your eTicket for any reason, simply print your Paypal receipt and bring it and/or some ID with you to the Pre-booked Tickets tent at the main entrance where we will be able to run through some security questions and issue your entry wristband(s).

Getting to Oceanfest

By Car | By Bus | By Foot | By Coach | By Taxi | By Train | By Plane

By Foot

When walking to and from the festival site, please do so QUIETLY and with due respect to the local inhabitants. Access down Moor Lane is especially narrow and it is advised that you walk in single file and take great care of vehicles. PLEASE do not consider short cuts back to your campsites or trespassing through private property.

By Car

North and South off the M5 Motorway at Junction-27

Follow the North Devon Link Road A361 (be careful, things can get a bit manic just when you think you're nearly there!)

Head for Barnstaple >; A361

For Croyde travel around Barnstaple using the "Downstream Bridge" and keep on the A361 taking the left at the traffic lights in Braunton and proceed on the B3226 to Croyde via Saunton.

Parking is available and operated by private companies, get there early to avoid disappointment and hassle.

For detailed 'turn by turn' directions to the Arena in Croyde enter EX33 1NZ as your destination in the AA route finder or on your GPS. Be careful to avoid the village of Georgeham in any options, it is a small village and doesn't cope well under excessive traffic.
