NicMods Classes

 

These classes are an opportunity for makers and artists alike to come and learn how to mould and cast their work. Learn from an experienced model maker and special effects technician, Nicole Saunders. She has worked on a number of films, including James Bond: Sky Fall, Les Miserables and Sherlock Holmes. You will discover by example and practice the skills needed to make moulds and casts of objects, Learning about materials and methods used in the film industry and how to transfer these skills to your own 3Dimensional pieces.

We shall be moulding an iconic prop, from a well known movie, as a group and casting out enough for everyone to take home a copy.

The format of the course will run much like a day in the life of a moulding workshop in the film industry. The course costs £180 per person for two days tuition and experience, this also includes, all the health and safety equipment, all the materials, and of course snacks!

It runs from 11am to 3pm on the 6th & 7th May, though it can overrun subject to the students availability and the amount of fun we’re having. It will be held at my workshop at The Old Saw Mill in Filleigh, North Devon. If you are interested please contact me directly here.

The Customisable Model Maker – Nic Saunders

Hi welcome to my site! I’m Nic. I’m a model maker and SFX technician for the film industry, I also make stunt armour for film and television.

I graduated from the University of Hertfordshire in 2010 with a first class degree in model design and model effects and promptly proceeded to jump into the film industry where I started as a model maker on Captain America. This was my first job working on a film production, but I have been making creating and designing my whole life and started taking on my first commissions whilst at North Devon college in 2007.

Since then I have worked on a number of films and made a whole load of cool projects! I now have my own fully equipped workshop in Filleigh, North Devon, and I have access to a polyurethane spray machine for customers interested in bespoke armour/costume.
