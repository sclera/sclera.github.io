Wedding Cake Ideas

Inspiration...

Hello!

What would be cool is if we have the three tiers sitting directly on top of each other, all covered in icing (probably white?), and then have sugar roses on the top and trailing down and round the whole cake. We could also have a row of sugar pearls around the base of each layer (where people sometimes put ribbon).

I think the pearls should be cream coloured and the roses could be a mixture of different pinks and purples. It looks as though you can get iridescent icing colours, which would be a cool way of finishing the pearls and the roses!

What do you think?

If the roses are hard to make, I think we could either get a little team of people together to do it in advance or we could choose a simpler type of flower (or even just use real flowers if it's that bad!).
