My name is Will Smith and I have been house and pet sitting in the North Devon area for four years.

My family and I have always lived in Swimbridge, a beautiful country village just outside of Barnstaple. Growing up so close to seaside towns such as Woolacombe, Braunton and Croyde, I developed a strong passion for surfing. So, after finishing college, I traveled to the world with my surfboards visiting Indonesia, Africa and the Caribbean.

Since settling back in Devon I have built up a strong reputation with both locals and holiday makers alike as a highly responsible and reliable house/pet sitter. It all started with looking after pets of friends whom didn't like the idea of putting them in into kennels whilst they were on holiday.

I have worked hard to build my reputation and as the testimonials show people not only rely on my service, they fully trust me and chose me time and time again.
