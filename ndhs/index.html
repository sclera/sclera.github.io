Here at North Devon House Sitter, I aim to offer you a wide range of services, everything from a basic ‘house checker’ service to a 4 week pet sit.

Whether it’s preparing your meals for your first few nights, purchasing the essentials, putting on the heating, making up the beds with fresh sheets or simply making sure you locked the back door in the mad rush to leave. I have you covered.

I offer very competitive rates for pet sitting, only a little more than the average kennel/cattery and your beloved pet gets to stay in the comfort of its own home, walked in its usual surroundings and have cuddles before bed.

All prices are flexible and vary from one person to the next, so please feel free to contact me and we can discuss your specific needs and come to some arrangement :)
